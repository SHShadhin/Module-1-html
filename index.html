The Winter Morning

This morning felt a little different. Looking out the window, I saw everything wrapped in mist. It was so cold that even at 11 AM, it felt like dawn was still far away.

You know, on such misty mornings, getting out of bed feels like the hardest thing to do. Does that ever happen to you? It certainly does to me. I just wanted to stay curled up in bed all day. And how perfect it would have been if a steaming mug of coffee magically appeared within arm's reach! Even better, if someone sat beside me and read a book aloud. In South Asia, we don't have snowfall like Europe or America. But our winter mornings are so charming that if you ever visit, you'll surely enjoy them. The surroundings are often blanketed with mist, and the cold air makes your breath visible as little puffs of steam.In the countryside, these winter mornings are even more delightful. Walking through the village roads, you'll spot earthen pots tied to date trees, waiting to collect sweet date sap.
